About Me

Hello! My name is Jacob Higham! I was born on Febuary 24th, 1998 in Sacramento,CA.

I served three years in the U.S. Army's 25th Infantry Division as a rifleman and Radiotelephone Operator. I was stationed in Schofield Barracks, Hawaii and was assigned to 2nd Battalion, 35th Infantry Regiment, Alpha Company 2nd Platoon. I finished my service as a Sergeant after recieving the promotion in the inactive reserves.

After finishing my service in the military, I earned my Emergency Medical Technician license in 2019 and provided 911 services to Northern California, working mainly in the Sacramento and Stockton areas. I specialized in traumatic injuries and also served as a Field Training Officer for new and student EMTs.

While working as an EMT, I started work on an International Studies degree and am conversationaly fluent in Japanese! まだまだ学ぶことがたくさんある！

Currently, I'm learning to code through the bootcamp at UC Davis, I've found that I have a love of Web Development and am looking forward to advancing and getting better at it!
